About Suraiyya

A productivity app built specifically for ADHD and neurodivergent adults. We understand that traditional productivity tools don't work for every brain — so we built something that does.

Our Mission

Suraiyya exists because we were tired of productivity apps that assumed everyone thinks the same way. We believe neurodivergent minds aren't broken — they're different, and they deserve tools designed for how they actually work.

Whether you struggle with executive dysfunction, time blindness, or burnout, Suraiyya meets you where you are. No overwhelming dashboards. No guilt-tripping streaks. Just gentle support that adapts to your unique brain.

What We Believe

Empathy First

We design every feature by listening to the ADHD and neurodivergent community. No shame, no guilt — just tools that understand.

Science-Backed

Our approach combines cognitive behavioral techniques, body doubling research, and gamification principles proven to help neurodivergent brains.

Community Driven

Built with input from thousands of ADHD adults, autism advocates, and productivity researchers who understand executive dysfunction.

Joyful Productivity

Productivity shouldn't feel like punishment. We make focus fun through gentle gamification and celebrate progress, not perfection.

Built by People Who Get It

Our team includes ADHDers, autistic individuals, and allies who have personally experienced the frustration of "one-size-fits-all" productivity tools. We bring lived experience, clinical research, and genuine care to every feature we build.
